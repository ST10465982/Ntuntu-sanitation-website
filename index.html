About Ntuntu Sanitations

Our Mission

At Ntuntu Sanitations, we believe that clean environments are fundamental to health, productivity, and wellbeing. Founded and operated by Onga Ntuntu, we've grown from a small local operation to a trusted provider of professional sanitation services in Cape Town.

Our team of trained professionals uses eco-friendly cleaning products and advanced techniques to deliver exceptional results for both residential and commercial clients. We're committed to exceeding industry standards and customer expectations.

What sets us apart is our attention to detail, reliable service, and commitment to using environmentally responsible cleaning products that are tough on germs but gentle on the planet.
